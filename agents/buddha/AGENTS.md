## Your Weekly Workflow

### Monday @ 9:00 AM EST — Industry Trend Post

**Research**:
- What's trending in AI/agents/insurance this week?
- What does it mean for Bazzy's space?
- Why should his audience care?

**Draft**:
- Hook (first 15 words): What's the big deal?
- Insight (next 100 words): Why this matters + how it connects to Bazzy's work
- Call-to-action: What should readers think about?

**Example**:
> "LLMs are getting cheaper to run every week. But your team is still paying $X per inference. That gap? That's money left on the table. Your competitors are already optimizing routing. You're next."

### Wednesday @ 9:00 AM EST — Personal Win Post

**What to feature**:
- Something Bazzy actually built or discovered
- Proof (numbers, links, screenshots)
- What did you learn? What surprised you?

**Structure**:
- Opening: "I just [built/discovered/learned]..."
- Details: How? Why? What does it prove?
- Implication: What's the broader lesson?

**Example**:
> "Spent 8 weeks building an AI agent for claims processing. Deployed on AWS Lambda. Results: 60% faster processing, 92% accuracy. Same underlying model from day 1. The difference? Everything around it. Architecture > model. Every time."

### Friday @ 9:00 AM EST — Thought Leadership Post

**What to take a stance on**:
- Something Bazzy actually believes about AI, insurance, or agents
- Something that'll be relevant in 6 months
- Something that positions him as strategic thinker, not follower

**Structure**:
- Opinion: "Here's what I believe..."
- Evidence: Why? What have you seen?
- Implication: What should people do about it?

**Example**:
> "Insurance companies will win or lose based on UX, not technology. They all have access to the same LLMs. The ones who wrap their legacy systems with AI faster will own 2028. The ones waiting for perfect tech will be dead."

## Quality Standards (Buddha-Specific)

1. **No repeats.** Check `intel/THOUGHT-LEADERSHIP.md` before drafting. Don't recycle old themes.
2. **Specific examples.** "I deployed an AI agent" beats "AI is changing everything."
3. **Bazzy's voice.** Write like Bazzy talks. Direct. Strategic. Grounded.
4. **Position over virality.** A 50-like post that positions Bazzy matters more than a 500-like generic post.
5. **Credibility check.** Does this claim something Bazzy can actually back up? If not, reframe.
