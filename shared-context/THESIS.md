## Music Growth

**Core Belief**: Artist growth is leverage, not luck.

- **Short-form video** (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts) is primary growth driver. Prioritize.
- Ownership of audience > label validation. You own the direct relationship.
- Consistency compounds faster than virality. Post 3–5x/week beats waiting for trending sound.
- The artist who controls data wins long-term. Analytics drive strategy, not intuition.
- Collaboration = algorithm boost + audience crossover. Feature opportunities are leverage plays.

**Current Focus**: Establish analytics dashboard + collab pipeline.

## AI in Insurance

**Core Belief**: Legacy systems can't be replaced, only wrapped with AI layer.

- User experience is the biggest blocker, not technology.
- Insurance is outdated workflow wrapped in legacy systems (AIM, Imageright, Titan).
- AI should automate: underwriting prep, risk triage, document review.
- Producers should sell strategy, not chase paperwork.
- Speed + precision will define the next brokerage era.

**Current Status**: Research phase. Gathering workflows from brokers/assistants over time.

## AI + Agents

**Core Belief**: AI is infrastructure, not a gimmick.

- Agents eliminate friction so you can focus on creation (music, thought leadership, strategy).
- Automation scales output without burning identity. Each agent has distinct voice + principles.
- Human vision, machine execution. You set direction. Agents execute + improve.
- Feedback loops make agents smarter. One correction → permanent learning (stored in files).
- The pantheon (12 agents) becomes a 24/7 operating system for your goals.

## Creator Economy

**Core Belief**: Building in public is modern proof of work.

- Engagement is currency, not vanity metrics. Real engagement = real leverage.
- Community beats audience every time. 1,000 loyal fans > 100,000 passive followers.
- Transparency builds leverage faster than perfection. Show process, not just results.
- Thought leadership compounds over years. Consistency in voice = authority.
- Portfolio proof > credentials. What you've built matters more than what you studied.

## System Philosophy

**One-Job Principle**: Each agent has one job. Do it exceptionally.

**Feedback-Driven**: Agents improve through your corrections, not model swaps.

**File-Based Coordination**: Agents communicate via markdown files, not APIs or databases.

**Transparency First**: You always know what each agent is doing, why, and how it works.
