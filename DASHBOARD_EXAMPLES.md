## What These Examples Show You

1. **Daily Clarity**: You'll know exactly what's working and what's not
2. **Action Items**: Clear recommendations (post when, what format, which platform)
3. **Cross-Platform View**: See how TikTok growth boosts Spotify, YouTube, Instagram
4. **Early Warnings**: If a platform dips, you'll know immediately
5. **Trend Spotting**: Dashboard identifies trending sounds before they peak
6. **Growth Tracking**: Week-to-week, month-to-month momentum visible
7. **Forecasting**: Predictions based on current velocity

## When Dashboard Launches

- **Day 1**: Baseline snapshot (current followers, views, etc.)

- **Week 2**: Patterns visible (best posting times, formats, platforms)
- **Week 3**: Recommendations get smarter (based on your real data)
- **Week 4+**: Predictive insights (what will work based on patterns)

This becomes your **daily strategic guide**. Instead of guessing, you'll have data.
